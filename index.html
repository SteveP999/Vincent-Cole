About the Artist

Vincent Cole

Vincent Cole inhabits a world where the orchestra never stops playing and the night never quite ends. Drawing from the golden age of American song — big band arrangements, lush strings, the smoky swagger of the supper club — Cole brings a voice that feels like a discovery and a memory at the same time. His debut record, The Cole Standard, is an invitation back to the room where the music was everything.

A Hello Texas Records artist.
